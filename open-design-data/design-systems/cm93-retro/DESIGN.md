# CM93 Retro

> Category: Custom
> Surface: web

Authentic Championship Manager 93 Amiga/PC aesthetic — extracted from actual game screenshots. Yellow title bars, blue panels, grey content areas, 1px beveled borders, flat solid colors, Amiga Topaz-style pixel font.

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere

Retro 1993 Amiga/PC football management game. Text-heavy, data-dense, functional. Feels like an early 90s DOS application with bright yellow title bars, deep blue panels, and light grey content areas. Every pixel serves a purpose — no decorative flourishes.

## 3. Typography

- Body: VT323 / Courier New, monospace, 16px, regular weight, 1.25 line-height
- Heading 1: VT323 / Courier New, monospace, 24px, regular weight
- Stat value: VT323 / Courier New, monospace, 16px, regular weight
- Stat label: VT323 / Courier New, monospace, 10px, regular weight
- All-caps for headers and buttons
- No bold or italic — pure bitmap font style

## 4. Spacing

- xs: 4px, sm: 8px, md: 12px, lg: 16px, xl: 24px
- Border radius: 0px (sharp corners)

## 5. Layout & Composition

- Single-column mobile-first layout, max-width 430px
- Text-heavy, data-dense display
- The green football pitch is the primary game area
- Flat colored panels with 1px beveled borders (gadtools style)

## 6. Components

- Title bar: Gold (#E0E060) background, brown (#403010) text, all-caps, 1px bottom border
- Button: Light grey (#A5A4A3) background, black text, 2px beveled borders (light top/left, dark bottom/right)
- Panel: Light blue (#2080C0) background, white text, 1px borders
- List item: Light blue (#2080C0) background, white text, 1px borders, yellow highlight on select
- Stat box: Light blue (#2080C0) background, white value text, 1px borders
- Group header: Deep blue (#0040A0) background, white text, all-caps
- Field slot: Dark green (#00A040) background, white text, 1px borders
- Position badge: Deep blue (#0040A0) background, white text, 1px borders

## 7. Motion & Interaction

- No transitions or animations
- Button press inverts the beveled border (dark becomes light, light becomes dark)
- Click states: highlight with yellow (#ECEB65)
- No hover states (mouse-driven, not touch)

## 8. Voice & Brand

- All-caps for headers and buttons
- Mixed case for data values
- Functional, no-nonsense tone
- Pixel-perfect retro aesthetic

## 9. Anti-patterns

- No gradients
- No rounded corners
- No shadows or drop shadows
- No decorative elements
- No animations or transitions
- No dark overlays or scanlines (clean pixel art)
- No modern design patterns (cards, floating buttons, etc.)
- Maximum 32 colors on screen at once
