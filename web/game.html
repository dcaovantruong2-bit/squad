📖 How to Play SQUAD
📋
1. Build Your Squad
Pick 11–12 players within a 360 budget. Each player has five stats — ATK, PAC, PAS, DEF, SPC — plus unique traits (pacey, leader, technical, etc.). Traits unlock squad-wide persistent synergies that last the whole match. Choose wisely — every player counts.
🏗️
2. Pick a Formation
Formations give position bonuses (+chips) to certain roles and a global multiplier to all scores. Browse the carousel and pick the one that best fits your squad — a full 11-slot match gives the best results. Some formations boost certain positions at the cost of others.
🃏
3. Choose Phases
Each round you're dealt 6 phase cards. Pick 3 to play, and order matters — combo chains between consecutive phases (like Defensive → Transition) trigger bonus ×mult or +chips effects. Plan your sequence for maximum scoring.
✨
4. Trigger Synergies
Pairing players by position, stats, and traits unlocks powerful synergies during phases — extra chips, add_mult, and ×mult bonuses. Stack them high, clear each round's target, and win 2 out of 3 rounds per match to advance through 5 matches to the final.
