HOW TO PLAY
1. SQUAD

Build your 11-player starting XI from a pool of 36 cards across 4 tiers:

  • Gold — Star players (limited)
  • Purple — Key contributors
  • Blue — Solid picks
  • Grey — Depth & budget
2. FORMATIONS

Choose from formations like 4-3-3, 4-4-2, 3-5-2. Each formation sets position requirements and influences chemistry bonuses between adjacent players.

3. PHASES & SYNERGIES

Matches play across 13 tactical phases (Attack, Midfield, Defense, Set Pieces). Each phase, cards trigger abilities and synergies. Stack players with matching traits for multiplier chains.

4. CAMPAIGN

Survive a 5-match campaign. After each match:

    <
  • Replace underperformers with bench players
  • Draw new cards from the pool
  • Adapt your strategy to the next opponent

Lose 3 matches and your campaign ends.
